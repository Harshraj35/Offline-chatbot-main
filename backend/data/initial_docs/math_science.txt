# Math and Science Fundamentals

## Mathematics
- Pythagorean Theorem: a² + b² = c² (for right-angled triangles)
- Quadratic Formula: x = [-b ± sqrt(b² - 4ac)] / 2a
- Area of Circle: A = πr²
- Order of Operations: PEMDAS (Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplication/Division, Addition/Subtraction)

## Science
- Newton's Laws of Motion:
  1. Object stays at rest unless acted upon.
  2. F = ma (Force equals mass times acceleration).
  3. For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.
- Photosynthesis: Plants convert sunlight, water, and CO2 into glucose and oxygen.
- DNA: Deoxyribonucleic acid, the molecule that carries genetic information.
- Atomic Structure: Protons and neutrons in the nucleus, electrons in orbits.
