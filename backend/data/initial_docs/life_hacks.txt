# Life Hacks and Problem Solving

## Problem Solving Framework
1. Define the problem clearly.
2. Brainstorm all possible solutions.
3. Evaluate each solution (pros and cons).
4. Implement the best solution.
5. Review the result and adjust.

## Productivity Hacks
- 5-Second Rule: If you have an impulse to act on a goal, you must physically move within 5 seconds or your brain will kill the idea.
- Eat the Frog: Do your hardest task first thing in the morning.
- Two-Minute Rule: If a task takes less than two minutes, do it immediately.
- Batching: Group similar tasks together (e.g., answering emails all at once).

## Financial Basics
- Budgeting: Track your income and expenses.
- Emergency Fund: Save 3-6 months of living expenses.
- Compound Interest: Start investing early for long-term growth.
